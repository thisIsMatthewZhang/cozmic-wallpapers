Support

Help with Cozmic Wallpapers.

For app support, purchase questions, privacy requests, or account help, use the support contact listed in the Cozmic Wallpapers app store listing.

What to include

When contacting support, include the device platform, app version if available, a short description of the issue, and any relevant purchase or generation details. Do not include sensitive passwords or payment card information.

Privacy and deletion requests

You can request access, correction, deletion, or export of eligible personal information. Some purchase, refund, fraud-prevention, tax, accounting, or legal records may need to be retained where required.

Review the Privacy Policy for more information.

Purchases and credits

Cozmic Wallpapers uses a credit-based system for generation. If a purchase does not appear correctly, include the platform and approximate purchase time when contacting support.
