Support

Help with Cozmic Wallpapers.

For app support, purchase questions, privacy requests, or account help, contact Cozmic Wallpapers support and include only the details needed to understand your request.

Contact support

Email: cozmicwallpapers@gmail.com

What to send

To help us respond quickly, include the smallest useful set of details for your issue:

  • Your device platform, such as iPhone or iPad.
  • Your app version, if available.
  • The email address on your Cozmic Wallpapers account.
  • A short description of what happened and what you expected.
  • For purchase or credit issues, the approximate purchase date and credit pack (Planet, Star, Galaxy, or Universe).
  • For generation issues, the approximate time of the generation and selected resolution.

Do not send passwords, full payment card numbers, or other sensitive information.

Privacy and deletion requests

You can request access, correction, deletion, or export of eligible personal information. Some purchase, refund, fraud-prevention, tax, accounting, or legal records may need to be retained where required.

For privacy requests, include the email address on your Cozmic Wallpapers account and the type of request you are making.

Review the Privacy Policy for more information.

Purchases and credits

Cozmic Wallpapers uses a credit-based system for generation. If a purchase does not appear correctly, include your platform, approximate purchase time, and the credit pack you attempted to buy. Payment details are handled by the app store and should not be sent to support.
